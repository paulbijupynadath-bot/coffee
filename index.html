our story

A little shop with a big heart

from bean to cup

Brew & Bloom started in 2015 as a tiny corner shop with one espresso machine and a whole lot of passion . Today we source our beans directly from farmers in kerala ,Ethiopia, and colombia,ensuring every cup supports sustainable farming.


We believe coffee is more than a drink- it is a moment of connection. Come pull up a chair ,slow down , and let us brew something memorable for you
